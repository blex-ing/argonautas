How the game would work

This flowchart shows what would be the actions or decisions one mission should follow. We made an example of Apollo 11’s mission. We made the sample questions it would ask you, taking into account what actually happened in the real mission and using open sources NASA provided about that specific mission and its respective consequences and outcome of your decision. Also, the consequences have to be realistic as our main objective is to make our audience learn while playing so all the information used and explanations had to be realistic and reliable. Also, we want to add a crisis question with a countdown in each mission to imitate a real-life situation in a space operation where you must improvise if something goes wrong and encourage problem-solving abilities.
